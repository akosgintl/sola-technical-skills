# AI Governance Frameworks

> [!summary]
> Structured frameworks — such as the EU AI Act and NIST AI RMF — for managing AI risk, accountability, and transparency across an organization's AI systems.

**Domain:** [[tier-1-edge|Security & Compliance]]

## What it is

AI governance frameworks provide a structured way to identify, assess, and mitigate the risks of AI systems and to assign accountability for them. They define risk tiers, documentation and transparency duties, and oversight processes. Examples include the EU AI Act's risk-based obligations and the NIST AI Risk Management Framework. For an architect they translate into concrete design and documentation requirements.

## Key concepts

- Risk-tiering of AI systems
- NIST AI RMF and EU AI Act
- Transparency and documentation duties
- Accountability and oversight roles
- Model and use-case inventories
