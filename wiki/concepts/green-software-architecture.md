# Green Software Architecture

> [!summary]
> Designing and operating software to minimize energy use and carbon emissions, treating sustainability as a measurable, first-class architectural quality.

**Priority:** 🟢 P3 · **Domain:** [[tier-3-watch|Emerging & Adjacent]] · **Roadmap:** §9.7

## What it is

Green software architecture applies efficiency and carbon-awareness to system design. It seeks to do more useful work per unit of energy, shift flexible workloads to times and regions with cleaner electricity (carbon-aware scheduling), and measure impact with standardized metrics. It overlaps strongly with cost optimization, since efficient systems are usually cheaper too.

## Key concepts

- Energy efficiency and carbon-awareness
- Software Carbon Intensity (SCI) metric
- Carbon-aware scheduling (time/region shifting)
- Demand shaping and hardware efficiency
- Overlap with cost optimization
