# AI Data Fabric

> [!summary]
> The unified data layer that feeds AI and ML systems — connecting vector stores, feature stores, and operational data into a coherent, governed substrate for inference and training.

**Domain:** [[tier-2-solid|Data Architecture]]

## What it is

An AI data fabric integrates the specialized data infrastructure that modern AI applications depend on: embeddings and vector search for retrieval, feature stores for consistent training/serving data, and pipelines connecting them to governed enterprise data. It is the data-architecture answer to the question "how do we reliably feed our models?"

## Key concepts

- Vector and embedding stores ([[vector-and-embedding-stores]])
- Feature stores ([[feature-stores]])
- Retrieval pipelines for RAG and agents
- Training/serving consistency
- Governance and lineage over AI data
