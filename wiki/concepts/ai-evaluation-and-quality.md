# AI Evaluation and Quality

> [!summary]
> The pipelines and metrics for measuring whether an AI system is good enough and staying that way — covering offline evals, online monitoring, drift, and hallucination detection.

**Priority:** 🟠 P1 · **Domain:** [[tier-1-edge|AI & Agentic Architecture]] · **Roadmap:** §1.5

## What it is

AI evaluation and quality is the systematic measurement of an AI system's correctness, safety, and reliability. It spans curated test sets and LLM-as-judge scoring offline, plus production monitoring for drift, regression, and hallucination online. Without an eval harness, changes to prompts, models, or retrieval are guesses; with one, quality becomes a tracked, defensible metric.

## Key concepts

- Eval pipelines and benchmark sets
- LLM-as-judge scoring
- Drift and regression detection
- Hallucination measurement
- Online vs. offline evaluation
