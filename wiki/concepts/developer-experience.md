# Developer Experience

> [!summary]
> The discipline of reducing friction for engineers through golden paths, self-service platforms, and paved-road tooling that make the right thing the easy thing.

**Priority:** 🟡 P2 · **Domain:** [[tier-2-solid|Platform Engineering & IaC]] · **Roadmap:** §4.3

## What it is

Developer Experience (DevEx) is the platform-engineering goal of letting teams ship safely without deep infrastructure expertise. It is delivered through Internal Developer Platforms (IDPs) that expose curated, opinionated "golden paths" — pre-approved templates, workflows, and abstractions — while keeping guardrails enforced underneath.

## Key concepts

- Golden paths / paved roads
- Internal Developer Platforms (IDPs), e.g. Backstage
- Self-service provisioning and templating
- Cognitive load reduction; platform-as-product mindset
- DevEx metrics (lead time, deployment frequency, time-to-first-commit)
