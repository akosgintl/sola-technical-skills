# Cloud Governance at Scale

> [!summary]
> The frameworks and controls — landing zones, Well-Architected reviews, and preventive guardrails — that keep a large, multi-account cloud estate secure, compliant, and cost-controlled.

**Priority:** 🟠 P1 · **Domain:** [[tier-1-edge|Cloud Architecture]] · **Roadmap:** §2.4

## What it is

Cloud governance at scale provides the guardrails for many teams operating in many accounts. Landing zones establish a standardized, secure account structure; Well-Architected frameworks give review lenses for reliability, security, cost, and performance; and policy-driven guardrails prevent misconfiguration automatically. The aim is to let teams move fast within safe boundaries rather than gating everything centrally.

## Key concepts

- Landing zones and account structure
- Well-Architected framework reviews
- Preventive vs. detective guardrails
- Policy-as-code and config rules
- Cost and security controls at scale
