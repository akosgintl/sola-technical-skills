# Data Governance and Lineage

> [!summary]
> The policies, catalogs, contracts, and tracking that ensure data is discoverable, trustworthy, compliant, and traceable from origin to consumption.

**Domain:** [[tier-2-solid|Data Architecture]]

## What it is

Data governance establishes ownership, access, quality, and compliance rules for data assets. Lineage tracks how data moves and transforms across systems, supporting impact analysis and audit. Data contracts formalize producer/consumer expectations; catalogs make assets discoverable with rich metadata.

## Key concepts

- Data catalogs and metadata management
- Data contracts (schema, SLAs, semantics)
- Lineage tracking and impact analysis
- Data quality dimensions and validation
- Access governance and regulatory compliance
