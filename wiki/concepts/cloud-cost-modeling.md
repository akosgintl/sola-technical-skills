# Cloud Cost Modeling

> [!summary]
> Building cost estimates and models alongside architecture designs so spend becomes a first-class, predictable design dimension rather than a surprise.

**Domain:** [[tier-2-solid|FinOps & Cost Architecture]]

## What it is

Cloud cost modeling is the practice of projecting the financial impact of architectural choices before and during build. It maps workloads to pricing models (on-demand, reserved, spot, committed use), accounts for data transfer and storage tiers, and produces unit-economics estimates that inform trade-offs. It is the analytical core of FinOps.

## Key concepts

- Pricing models: on-demand, reserved, spot, committed-use
- Unit economics and cost-per-transaction
- Egress, storage tiers, and hidden cost drivers
- Cost estimation alongside design (TCO)
- FinOps operating model and accountability
