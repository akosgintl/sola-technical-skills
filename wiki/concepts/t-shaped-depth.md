# T-Shaped Depth

> [!summary]
> The skill profile of broad architectural literacy across many domains combined with genuine, hands-on depth in a few — the shape that lets an architect both connect systems and earn credibility.

**Domain:** [[meta-skills|Meta-Skills]]

## What it is

T-shaped depth describes wide horizontal breadth — enough fluency across domains to reason about a whole system — anchored by deep vertical expertise in a small number of areas. The breadth enables systems thinking and integration; the depth provides hard-won judgment and the credibility to make calls others trust. For 2026 architects, picking where to go deep is itself a strategic decision.

## Key concepts

- Horizontal breadth across domains
- Deep vertical expertise in a few areas
- Breadth enables systems thinking
- Depth earns credibility and judgment
- Choosing where to specialize
