# Delegate, Review, Own

> [!summary]
> The working model for collaborating with AI and teams: delegate execution, rigorously review the output, and retain personal accountability for the result.

**Domain:** [[meta-skills|Meta-Skills]]

## What it is

"Delegate, review, own" is the operating discipline for an era where AI and agents do much of the production work. You delegate the doing to a model, agent, or team member; you critically review what comes back rather than rubber-stamping it; and you own the outcome regardless of who produced it. It is the human counterpart to human-in-the-loop design.

## Key concepts

- Delegating execution to AI/agents/teams
- Critical review over rubber-stamping
- Retained personal accountability
- Calibrating trust by stakes
- Connection to human-in-the-loop gates
