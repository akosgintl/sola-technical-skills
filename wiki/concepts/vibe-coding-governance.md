# Vibe Coding Governance

> [!summary]
> The platform-level controls needed when significant portions of code and infrastructure are generated by AI in a fast, intent-driven ("vibe coding") workflow.

**Priority:** 🟢 P3 · **Domain:** [[tier-3-watch|Emerging & Adjacent]] · **Roadmap:** §9.1

## What it is

"Vibe coding" describes building software by steering AI generation through natural-language intent rather than writing code line by line. As a platform responsibility, governance means putting guardrails around non-deterministic generation: review gates, policy enforcement, provenance, and quality assurance so velocity does not outrun safety and accountability.

## Key concepts

- Non-deterministic generation as a platform concern
- Review gates and "delegate, review, own"
- Policy and quality enforcement on generated artifacts
- Provenance and auditability of AI output
- Guardrails against insecure or non-compliant code
